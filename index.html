What is The Advert Coin Experiment?
The Advert Coin Experiment is testing a radical hypothesis: what happens when you take 100% of a cryptocurrency's developer allocation and pour it entirely into social media advertising? No team tokens, no private sales - just pure marketing power.
How it works
Every token from the developer allocation is converted directly into advertising budget. We're running targeted campaigns across major platforms where mainstream users spend their time, testing if visibility alone can drive crypto adoption.

The big question
Can advertising alone create a successful cryptocurrency? Most crypto projects fail because they never reach mainstream awareness. By dedicating everything to advertising, we're testing if pure marketing power can drive mass adoption and success.
Why this matters
This experiment could change how new tokens approach launch strategy forever. If successful, it proves that distribution and visibility matter more than complex tokenomics. If it fails, we'll have valuable data about the limits of pure marketing in crypto.
Follow the journey
This is a live experiment with real money and real results. We're documenting every campaign, every metric, and every outcome. Win or lose, the crypto community will learn something valuable about the power of advertising in driving adoption.
