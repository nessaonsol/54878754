What is Growth Token?
Growth Token is a cryptocurrency that uses advertising as its primary growth mechanism. Instead of traditional marketing approaches, the developer allocation is dedicated entirely to advertising campaigns across major platforms.
How it works
The developer bag funds targeted advertising campaigns on platforms where mainstream users spend their time. This approach follows the successful model of coins like DOGE, focusing on mass adoption through visibility.

The strategy
By investing developer funds directly into advertising rather than personal enrichment, Growth Token aims to onboard mainstream users who wouldn't otherwise discover cryptocurrency. The goal is sustainable growth through increased visibility and adoption.
