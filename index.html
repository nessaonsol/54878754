The Original Useless Token

In 2017, long before "useless" tokens became a meme, one frustrated user had enough. After getting rugged by multiple crypto projects and losing money to scam after scam, they decided to channel their pure rage into something honest. Thus, "Another Useless Token" (AUT) was born - not as a joke, but as a middle finger to the entire crypto space. This wasn't about being trendy or ironic. This was raw anger tokenized on the blockchain. The creator wanted something that would never promise anything, never disappoint anyone, and never rug anyone because it was upfront about being completely useless from day one.
