Making a Difference for Our Furry Friends

The Dog Project is a revolutionary cryptocurrency initiative where 100% of all transaction fees are donated directly to dog charities worldwide. Every trade, every transaction, every interaction with our coin helps save and improve the lives of dogs in need.

How The Dog Project Works

1

Buy or Trade Our Coin

Purchase Dog Project tokens through our platform. Every transaction generates a small fee that goes directly to our charity fund.

2

Fees Are Collected

All transaction fees are automatically collected in a transparent smart contract wallet that can be audited by anyone.

3

Monthly Donations

Every month, 100% of collected fees are distributed to verified dog charities, shelters, and rescue organizations.

4

Transparency Reports

We publish detailed reports showing exactly where every dollar went, complete with photos and updates from the charities we support.
